About Me

                    
Hey! You know that friend who's obsessed with efficiency, the one who optimises everything from solving complex problems to finding the fastest route to lunch? Or that intern who'd rather figure it out alone than ask for help, even when the deadline is closing in? Yeah, that's me.

                    
My name is Okoro Ebubechwuku Prince. I'm a Computer Science student at Babcock University and probably one of the most curious people you'll ever meet. From AI, automation and games to graphic design, 3D rendering and anime, if it's interesting, I'm already down the rabbit hole.

                    
I care deeply about how things look and how they work under the hood. I don't know exactly where I'll land yet, but I'm building, exploring and figuring it out one project at a time. The future looks bright.
